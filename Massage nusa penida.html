"Learning to surf requires a combination of passion, practice, and patience. Balangan Beach in Bali is not just a surf spot; it's a paradise where the waves meet the shore with a gentle rhythm, perfect for both beginners and experienced surfers alike. At the Milan club on Balangan Beach, we believe in teaching surfing as an art of finding balance, both on the board and in life. Our instructors focus on the fundamentals, helping you understand the waves, positioning, and the art of standing up on the board. The beauty of Balangan Beach adds to the experience, with its golden sands and crystal-clear waters. It's not just about surfing here; it's about connecting with nature and embracing the freedom that comes with riding the waves. Whether you're a beginner looking to catch your first wave or an experienced surfer seeking to improve your technique, the Milan club on Balangan Beach is the perfect place to learn and grow in your surfing journey.
Join us and discover the joy of surfing in one of Bali's most beautiful and welcoming surf spots.
We work from 8am until 8pm.
